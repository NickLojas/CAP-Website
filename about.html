About Me

My name is Bozena Tutaj. For over 20 years, I have been providing counselling and psychotherapy to individuals, couples and families dealing with emotional and behavioural problems, trauma, relationship difficulties, parenting issues and family breakdown. I am registered with the College of Registered Psychotherapists of Ontario and I am a member of the Ontario Association of Counsellors, Consultants, Psychometrists and Psychotherapists.
